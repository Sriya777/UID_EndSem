Description

Centella asiatica, commonly known as Mandukaparni or Gotu Kola, is a native Indian perennial herb. It is characterized by its small, green, fan-shaped leaves and is renowned for its potential cognitive-enhancing properties. Mandukaparni has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to promote mental clarity and calmness.
